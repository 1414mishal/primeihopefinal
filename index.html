OUR CARE SERVICES

New Standards in Dental Care

Digital Outlook

3D visualisation of your teeth using state-of-the-art intraoral scanners and advanced radio imaging.

Hollywood Smile

Personalized cosmetic smile designing and makeovers, using premium ceramics and composite veneers harmonizing your natural aesthetics.

Microscopic Endodontics

In-house single visit root canal specialists using microscopic loups for precise and hassle-free accurate treatment.

Digital Implants

Restoring missing teeth with digitally guided single and full mouth, dental implant placements, including full arch placements with the help of CBCT as well as intraoral scans. Restoring functionally balanced.

Orthodontics

Alignment of crooked and malaligned teeth with Invisalign and invisible braces as well as conventional orthodontics.

Expert Surgical Care

Procedures which includes impacted 3rd molar extractions, cyst enucleations and apicectomies, flap surgery, receding gum line corrections.

Single Day Ceramics

Immediate results with single day, Dental ceramics and CAD mill zirconia crowns, which are delivered to you within 24 hours to respect your busy schedule. And insured zirconia crown deliveries with warranties.

Preventive & Paediatric Dentists

Pediatric, restorative and preventive care with specialized dentists.
